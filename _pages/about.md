I am a first-year M.Eng. student in Mechanics at the [University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/), advised by [Assoc. Prof. Fenghua Qin](https://en.mecheng.ustc.edu.cn/). My research focuses on the **propulsion hydrodynamics of tandem undulating fins**, combining computational fluid dynamics (CFD) and experimental methods to understand the biologically inspired fin propulsion mechanisms.

Prior to USTC, I obtained my B.Eng. in Naval Architecture & Ocean Engineering from [Harbin Engineering University (HEU)](https://english.heu.edu.cn/) in 2024, graduating as an **Outstanding Graduate** with a GPA of 89.1/100 (Rank 13/191).

## Research Interests

- Biologically inspired propulsion (undulating fins, fish-like locomotion)
- Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) — Lattice Boltzmann, OpenLB
- Hydrodynamic performance analysis of marine structures
- Wave loads on multi-hull vessels
