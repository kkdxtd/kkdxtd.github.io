This paper investigates the propulsion hydrodynamics of **tandem undulating fin** configurations, drawing inspiration from the ribbon-fin locomotion observed in weakly electric fish (*Apteronotus albifrons*) and similar species.

**Key topics covered:**
- Effect of fin spacing and phase difference on thrust and efficiency
- Wake interaction patterns between leading and trailing fins
- Comparison with single-fin baseline performance
- Parametric analysis using 2D/3D CFD (OpenLB framework)

**Status:** In preparation — *Ocean Engineering* (Elsevier), expected 2026.
